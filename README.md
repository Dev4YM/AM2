# AM2

Open-source B2B sales intelligence platform: business discovery by location, AI review insights, personalized outreach, and GPS-mapped CRM with routes and team workflows.

## Important limitation

AM2 does **not** ship a proprietary real-time business database. Search and enrichment rely on configurable external data sources and your own stored leads. Plan integrations and quotas accordingly.
